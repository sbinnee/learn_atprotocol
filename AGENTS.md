# Learn AT Protocol

Goal: Study and document AT Protocol to understand its architecture, concepts, and implementation.

## Study Q&A Branch

The `study-qa` branch is dedicated to tracking questions and answers about AT Protocol.

### Instructions for Q&A Tracking

When a question about AT Protocol is asked:
1. Track the question and its answer in `QA.md`
2. Format each entry with:
   - Date
   - Question
   - Detailed answer with references/links where applicable
3. Keep entries in chronological order (newest at the top)
4. Tag entries with relevant topics (e.g., `#architecture`, `#lexicon`, `#pds`, etc.)

This creates a searchable knowledge base of AT Protocol concepts as we learn.
